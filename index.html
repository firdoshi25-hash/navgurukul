Chocolate Cake

Description:

This is a soft, moist, and delicious chocolate cake that everyone will love!

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup flour
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup cocoa powder
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/4 cup butter

    • Steps:

  • Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F).
  • Mix flour, sugar, cocoa powder, and baking powder in a bowl.
  • Add eggs, milk, and melted butter; mix until smooth.
  • Pour the batter into a greased baking pan.
  • Bake for 25-30 minutes or until a toothpick comes out clean.
  • Let it cool, then serve and enjoy!
