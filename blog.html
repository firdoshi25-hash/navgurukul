Why I Like Doraemon

I like Doraemon because he is blue in color and very cute. He always helps his friend Nobita with amazing gadgets from the future. Blue is also my favorite color, which makes Doraemon my favorite character.


Fun Facts about Doraemon

  • Doraemon comes from the 22nd century.
  • He has a pocket full of futuristic gadgets.
  • His favorite food is Dorayaki (Japanese pancake).
